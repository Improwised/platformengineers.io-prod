What is the typical timeline for the assessment?

Can you explain the money-back guarantee?
If you are not satisfied with the assessment outcome, we will refund the full amount as an expression of our commitment to your satisfaction.
Is signing an NDA required, and why?
We respect the confidentiality of your infrastructure and proprietary information. As we will require access to your infrastructure and proprietary information so if you have your own NDA, feel free to share it with us, and we will reciprocate by signing it.
